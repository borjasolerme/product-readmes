## The features that actually matter

**Multi-input video generation**

We designed VideoTok to meet you where you are. Text-to-video: describe what you want and the AI builds it. Link-to-video: paste a product page and we generate promo content automatically. Image-to-video: we animate static visuals into clips. Audio-to-video: we sync video to voiceovers or music.

The thinking behind this was simple. You should be able to generate content from whatever assets you already have. Got a product page? Paste the URL. Have a script? Drop in the text. Great product photo? Turn it into a video. Each input type uses different AI models we selected specifically for that starting material.

**AI avatars with emotional range**

We built the avatar library to go way beyond digital talking heads. Each avatar supports emotion control. You can adjust tone from enthusiastic to conversational to authoritative. Pick avatars that match your target demographic. Create content that actually feels relatable to specific audiences.

The product integration feature is one we spent a lot of time on. Instead of avatars just talking at the camera, they can hold, use, or interact with your product in the video. Clone any voice and use it across all your content for consistent brand audio. We support 30+ languages with accurate lip-sync translation. For brands expanding internationally, this removes one of the biggest barriers to localized content: finding native-speaking presenters for each market. One video, thirty languages, consistent brand voice. That was a problem we saw over and over with our clients before we solved it.

**AI animation and consistent characters**

Create animated characters that stay visually consistent across multiple scenes and videos. Great fit for brands that use mascots, illustrated spokespeople, or stylized content. ## Practical tips for getting started

Here's what we tell every new user based on what we've seen work best.

**Start with link-to-video for quick wins**

If you have product pages, start by pasting URLs into the link-to-video feature. Fastest way to see what VideoTok produces with your actual products. Results often need minimal editing, and it gives you an immediate feel for the platform.

**Set up brand guidelines before creating content**

Take 15 minutes to configure your brand colors, fonts, voice tone, and visual style before generating your first batch. Every video after that automatically follows these settings. Saves you from repetitive adjustments. We put brand setup front and center for a reason.

**Use bulk creation for ad testing, not final production**

Bulk creation works best for generating a large number of variations to test on ad platforms. Let Facebook or Google's algorithms find the winners from a big creative pool, then refine those winners for scaled distribution. This is the workflow we designed it around.

**Clone your brand voice early**

If you have existing video or audio content with your brand's voice, clone it in VideoTok before creating anything else. Consistent audio branding across all your AI-generated content makes everything feel more polished. Trust us on this one.

**Test different avatar styles with your audience**

Don't assume which avatar or presenter style will work best. Create the same script with three or four different avatars and run them as an A/B test. Audience preferences vary a lot by market. We've seen results surprise even experienced marketers.

**Localize strategically**

The multilingual feature is tempting to go wild with. But start with your two or three highest-value markets rather than translating into every available language. Get the messaging right where it matters most before expanding.
